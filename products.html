FACTORY AUTOMATION

Mitsubishi Electric's automation and drive division provides a vast range of factory automation and processing technologies designed to enhance productivity and quality on the factory floor. Their comprehensive product lineup includes Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), human-machine interfaces (HMIs), and a variety of drive products like AC servos and inverters. These solutions are used across diverse industries such as automotive, food and beverage, and semiconductors to create reliable, flexible, and sustainable manufacturing systems. Delta VFDs (Variable Frequency Drives) and PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers) are core components of industrial automation solutions, designed to optimize motor control and machine operation. The VFDs efficiently manage motor speed and torque, leading to energy savings and enhanced process control. Concurrently, the PLCs serve as the reliable "brains" of the operation, executing logic for seamless monitoring and automated decision-making. Together, these robust and integrated Delta products provide a comprehensive and cost-effective system for improving productivity and efficiency across diverse industrial applications.
