Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your typical response time?

We respond to all inquiries within 4 business hours during weekdays. Emergency support is available 24/7 for existing AMC clients.

Do you provide on-site support?

Yes, we provide on-site support throughout Delhi NCR. Site visits can be scheduled by appointment for detailed project assessments.

What brands do you work with?

We work with leading automation brands including Siemens, Mitsubishi, Delta, Yaskawa, and Omron. We also provide customized solutions.

Do you provide AMC services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) services for all types of automation equipment with preventive and corrective maintenance.

How can I get a quote?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or emailing us with your project requirements and specifications.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Delhi and serve the entire Delhi NCR region. We also handle projects across India depending on the scope and requirements.
