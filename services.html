Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Our AMC services ensure your automation systems operate at peak efficiency with minimal downtime. We provide comprehensive preventive and corrective maintenance for all automation equipment.

Preventive Maintenance

  • Regular scheduled inspections
  • Component cleaning and calibration
  • Software updates and backups
  • Performance optimization
  • Wear and tear assessment
  • Spare parts replacement planning

Corrective Maintenance

  • Rapid fault diagnosis
  • Emergency repair services
  • Component replacement
  • System debugging and testing
  • Performance restoration
  • Documentation updates

Equipment Covered Under AMC

PLC Systems

Programmable Logic Controllers, I/O modules, communication modules, and related accessories.

VFD Systems

Variable Frequency Drives, soft starters, motor controllers, and power electronics.

Servo Systems

Servo motors, servo drives, encoders, and motion control equipment.

HMI & SCADA

Human Machine Interfaces, touch panels, SCADA systems, and monitoring equipment.
